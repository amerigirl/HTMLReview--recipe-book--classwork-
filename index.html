Tasty PB and Chocolate Cookies!

  • Total Time: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 24 cookies

Ingredients
½ cup (113 g) unsalted butter, melted
½ cup (133g) creamy peanut butter
¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
⅔ cup (134g) packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 large egg
½ teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
1 ¼ cups (155g) all-purpose flour
1 ¼ cups (213g) chocolate chips


Instructions:
  1. Mix or stir butter with peanut butter until smooth. Add brown sugar and granulated sugar and mix until creamy.
  2. Beat in vanilla, egg, baking soda, and salt. Mix in flour slowly until cookie dough forms. Stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Scoop 2 tablespoon sized cookie dough balls onto cookie sheets covered with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Chill 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  5. Press cookie dough balls flat with the tines of a fork.
  6. Bake cookies for 8-12 minutes, or until slightly brown on the bottom and the top just loses the wet cookie dough look.
  7. Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days or freeze for up to one month.

Air Fryer Broccoli Bites

  • Total Time: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 10 pieces

Ingredients
2 cups broccoli cooked and finely chopped
1 cup cheddar cheese shredded
1 cup Mozzarella cheese shredded (or shredded cheese of your choice)
4 oz Cream cheese softened
1 Egg large
½ teaspoon salt salt and pepper to taste
½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
½ teaspoon garlic powder
½ teaspoon paprika
1 cup Panko breadcrumbs or regular breadcrumbs


Instructions:
  1. Mix the chopped broccoli and cheese together in a large bowl. Add the egg, spices, and mix everything together with your hands until it forms a sticky mixture.
  2. Shape the Balls. Now, shape the mixture into small ball-sized shapes with your hands. Make each ball approximately 1 inch in diameter, but not bigger than 1 and ½ inches. Or else the balls my flatten into a patty. Tip. Using your finger push a hole in each ball and add a small cube of solid cheese, such as Velveeta, cream cheese, Mozzarella cheese, or Goat cheese. Then close the hole and continue rolling the broccoli appetizer into a ball shape. Although it is optional, this will create the oozing cheese effect with an incredible cheese pull. Coat in Panko. Roll each ball in Panko breadcrumbs and ensure that it is fully coated.
  3. Freeze the balls on a parchment-lined baking tray for 30 minutes to an hour. While this step is optional, this will insure that the balls will not fall apart when air frying.
  4. Preheat your air fryer to 400F. Don’t skip this step. It’s important that the Broccoli cheddar poppers will start cooking immediately in a very hot air fryer. Place each ball onto your greased air fryer tray, leaving enough space for the air to circulate around each piece. Lightly spray each ball with cooking spray.
  5. Set your air fryer to 400°F and cook the broccoli cheddar bites for 5 minutes on each side. This means flip each ball on the other side after 5 minutes. Tip: Use the help of two spatulas to gently flip them. Do not use tongs, as they may accidentally crush the balls.
  6. Once they are done, remove them from the air fryer (I use flat silicone spatula to gently lift each ball) and place them onto a plate.

Easy Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

  • Total Time: 1 hour and 5 minutes
  • Servings: 12 slices

Ingredients
4 overripe bananas
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs, room temperature
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips


Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x5 metal loaf pan; set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the bananas; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, use a hand mixer to cream together butter and sugars for 2 to 3 minutes, until well combined and fluffy (you can also use a stand mixer.)
  4. Add in the eggs, mashed banana and vanilla and mix well.
  5. Sift the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt into the wet ingredients, then fold in until just combined. Fold in 3/4 cup of the chocolate chips.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips on top. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until the bread is set and a knife inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean. Allow the bread to cool for at least 20 minutes before serving.
